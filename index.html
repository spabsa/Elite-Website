Our Services

Geo Spatial 3D Scanning

We use Matterport 3D technology to document the exact location and condition of contents and the property. This provides a high definition record that assists in more than just inventorying.

Inventory

EPC's skilled technicians use Contents Track, the industry standard inventory software for inventorying. This employs photographs to catalog all your possessions, and a QR code track all personal goods.

Content Restoration

EPC works with your insurance adjuster to assess the extent of the damage to your belongings and determine what can be repaired to pre-loss condition, economically of course.

Storage

We even offer emergency storage when the need for off-site storage arises. Our storage facilities are highly organized with numbered vaults that are climate controlled, insured, and secure.

Packback

Once the restoration work is complete just set a date and EPC's technician will bring back all your possessions. They even place everything where you'd like, how you'd like.

Content Cleaning

Smoke, water, and mold-contaminated items cannot be packed away. That’s why EPC separately categorized these items in the inventory system for cleaning before they’re packed for storage.

Custom Crate Engineering

EPC creates custom engineered crates for art, luxury furniture, antiques, and collectibles. We employ specialized packing materials and techniques to protect luxury items during on site or off site relocation.

Declared Value Coverage

EPC offers stated value coverage on a per-project basis because we’re not a moving company. We inventory your items and know their individual value surpasses weight.

Our Expertise

Property insurance losses

We work exclusively in properties that have experienced a loss covered by insurance or where the property owner is self insured. Our services are tailored to the unique demands of these situations, such as around the clock availability on a moment’s notice.

Specialized in Hazardous Environments

Property damage can result in a wide variety of hazards that must be considered before we perform our services. These include asbestos, carcinogens (following a fire), biological contamination (following a flood), and mold. Our technicians are provided with the training and equipment to identify and operate safely in the presence of these hazards.

Experience with Carriers

With our extensive knowledge of the claims settlement process, we serve the policyholder both as a source of information and an advocate to navigate this often confusing experience. Both our field and administrative teams are carefully trained to provide support and guidance.
